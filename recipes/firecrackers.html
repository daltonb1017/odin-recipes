Kentucky Firecrackers

Description

This recipe takes nasty, bland crackers that only old men eat and seasons them with ranch, red pepper flakes,and garlic. A delicious, easy-to-make snack!

Saltines by themselves are totally wack,this recipe turns them into something very addictive, so be careful!

Ingredients

  • Saltine Crackers
  • Ranch Seasoning Packet (Hidden Valley is the best)
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Garlic Powder
  • Canola Oil

Steps

  1. Mix the oil and seasonings in a small bowl
  2. Place the crackers in a sealable container
  3. Cover the crackers with the mixture and seal the container
  4. Turn the container over every 5 minutes 30 minutes or when the crackers look evenly coated
